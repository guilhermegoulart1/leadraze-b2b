# Getting Started

Welcome to GetRaze! This guide will help you set up your account and launch your first campaign.

## 1. Create Your Account

1. Visit [GetRaze](https://getraze.co) and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile information

## 2. Connect Your Channels

GetRaze supports multiple communication channels. Connect at least one to get started:

### Email
- Go to **Settings** > **Channels** > **Email**
- Connect your email provider (Gmail, Outlook, or custom SMTP)
- Verify your email domain for better deliverability

### WhatsApp
- Navigate to **Settings** > **Channels** > **WhatsApp**
- Scan the QR code with your WhatsApp Business app
- Wait for the connection to be established

### LinkedIn
- Go to **Settings** > **Channels** > **LinkedIn**
- Follow the authentication steps
- Grant the necessary permissions

## 3. Import Your Leads

There are several ways to add leads to GetRaze:

### CSV Import
1. Go to **Leads** > **Import**
2. Upload your CSV file
3. Map the columns to GetRaze fields
4. Review and confirm the import

### Manual Entry
1. Navigate to **Leads** > **Add Lead**
2. Fill in the lead information
3. Click **Save**

### Contact Lists
Organize your leads into lists for better management:
1. Go to **Contact Lists** > **Create List**
2. Name your list and add a description
3. Add leads manually or import from CSV

## 4. Create Your First AI Agent

AI Agents are the heart of GetRaze. They handle conversations with your leads automatically.

1. Go to **AI Agents** > **Create Agent**
2. Choose the agent type (Email, WhatsApp, or LinkedIn)
3. Configure the agent:
   - **Name** - Give your agent a recognizable name
   - **Persona** - Define how the agent should present itself
   - **Instructions** - Provide context about your business and goals
   - **Response Style** - Set the tone (professional, friendly, casual)
4. Save your agent

## 5. Launch Your First Campaign

Now you're ready to start reaching out to leads:

1. Navigate to **Campaigns** > **Create Campaign**
2. Select your target leads or contact list
3. Choose the channel(s) for activation
4. Assign the AI agent(s) to handle responses
5. Configure the campaign schedule
6. Review and launch!

## 6. Monitor & Manage Conversations

Once your campaign is running:

- Check the **Inbox** for incoming messages
- Review AI agent responses before they're sent (optional)
- Take over conversations manually when needed
- Track lead status and progression
