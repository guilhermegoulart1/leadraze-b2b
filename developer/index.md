## What is GetRaze?

GetRaze is an AI-powered lead generation platform that helps businesses automate their prospecting and outreach. With intelligent AI agents, you can engage with leads across multiple channels while maintaining personalized, human-like conversations.

### Key Capabilities

- **Lead Collection** - Import leads from CSV, manual entry, or automated collection
- **AI-Powered Engagement** - Agents that respond intelligently based on your business context
- **Multi-Channel Activation** - Email, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn in one unified platform
- **Conversation Management** - Centralized inbox for all your lead communications
- **Team Collaboration** - Assign leads and conversations to team members
- **Performance Analytics** - Track and optimize your outreach campaigns
