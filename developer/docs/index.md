# Welcome to GetRaze

GetRaze is an AI-powered lead generation platform that helps businesses automate their prospecting and outreach. With intelligent AI agents, you can engage with leads across multiple channels while maintaining personalized, human-like conversations.

## What is GetRaze?

GetRaze combines powerful automation with artificial intelligence to help you:

- **Generate Leads** - Find prospects on LinkedIn and Google Maps
- **Automate Outreach** - Send personalized messages at scale
- **Engage Intelligently** - AI agents that respond naturally
- **Close More Deals** - Track and manage your pipeline

## Key Features

### AI Agents
Configure intelligent agents that automatically engage with your leads using personalized messages tailored to your business context.

### Multi-Channel Outreach
Reach your prospects through Email, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn - all from one unified platform.

### Lead Management
Import, organize, and track your leads with powerful list management tools and pipeline visualization.

### Smart Conversations
AI-powered responses that understand context and maintain natural conversations with your leads.

### Analytics & Insights
Track performance, measure conversions, and optimize your outreach strategy with detailed analytics.
