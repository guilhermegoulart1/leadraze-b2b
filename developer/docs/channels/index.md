## Why Multi-Channel?

Different prospects prefer different communication methods:

- **LinkedIn**: Best for B2B, professional services, and networking
- **Email**: Great for formal communication and detailed information
- **WhatsApp**: Ideal for quick responses and informal markets

## Best Practices

### Diversify Your Outreach
- Don't rely on a single channel
- Use different channels for different stages
- Respect channel-specific etiquette

### Maintain Account Health
- Stay within recommended limits
- Monitor health scores regularly
- Address issues promptly

### Personalize Per Channel
- LinkedIn: Reference profile details
- Email: Use professional formatting
- WhatsApp: Keep it conversational
