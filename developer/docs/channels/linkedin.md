# LinkedIn Channel

LinkedIn is the primary channel for B2B outreach in GetRaze. Connect your LinkedIn account to search for prospects, run campaigns, and automate conversations.

## Connecting Your Account

### Step-by-Step

1. Navigate to **LinkedIn Channels** in the sidebar
2. Click **Connect Account**
3. Enter your LinkedIn credentials
4. Complete any verification steps
5. Wait for connection confirmation

::: warning
Use your own LinkedIn account. Using shared or fake accounts violates LinkedIn's terms of service and can result in account restrictions.
:::

## Configuring Daily Limits

### Accessing Limit Settings

1. Click the **Settings** icon on your account card
2. The Limit Configuration modal opens

### Limit Configuration

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Daily Limit Slider | Set your maximum daily invitations |
| Strategy Presets | Quick selection: Conservative, Moderate, Aggressive |
| Recommended Limit | System-suggested safe limit |
| Override Option | Set higher than recommended (requires reason) |

### Strategy Presets

| Strategy | Limit Range | Best For |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| Conservative | Low | New accounts, rebuilding health |
| Moderate | Medium | Established accounts, steady growth |
| Aggressive | High | Premium accounts, time-sensitive campaigns |

::: tip
Start conservative and increase gradually as your account health improves.
:::

### Limit Override

If you need to exceed the recommended limit:

1. Enable the override toggle
2. Provide a reason (required)
3. Acknowledge the risk
4. Save changes

::: warning
Overriding limits can negatively impact your account health. Use sparingly and monitor results closely.
:::

## Account Actions

### Refresh Account

Click the refresh button to:
- Update account data
- Sync invitation counts
- Recalculate health score

### Remove Account

1. Click the delete button
2. Confirm removal
3. Account is disconnected

::: info
Removing an account doesn't delete data from LinkedIn. It only disconnects from GetRaze.
:::

## Multiple Accounts

You can connect multiple LinkedIn accounts:

- Manage different market segments

### Account Selection in Campaigns

When creating campaigns, you'll select which LinkedIn account to use. Consider:
- Account type and limits
- Target audience alignment

   - Review and launch

## Best Practices

### Profile Optimization
- Complete your LinkedIn profile
- Use a professional photo
- Write a compelling headline
- Keep summary updated

### Connection Requests
- Personalize every message
- Reference something specific
- Keep it short (300 chars max)
- Provide clear value

### Activity Patterns
- Don't send all invitations at once
- Spread activity throughout the day
- Avoid weekends (optional)
- Match normal user behavior

### Building Trust
- Accept relevant incoming connections
- Engage with content
- Don't spam
- Respond to messages promptly

## Troubleshooting

### Account Won't Connect
- Verify credentials are correct
- Check for 2FA requirements
- Try from a different browser
- Contact support if persists

### Low Health Score
- Reduce daily limits
- Pause campaigns temporarily
- Review rejection rates
- Improve message quality

### Invitations Not Sending
- Check daily limit usage
- Verify account is active
- Review campaign status
- Check for LinkedIn restrictions

### Account Restricted
- Pause all campaigns immediately
- Follow LinkedIn's recovery steps
- Wait for restriction to lift
- Contact GetRaze support
