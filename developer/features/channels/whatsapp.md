# WhatsApp Channel

WhatsApp enables direct, personal communication with your leads. It's ideal for markets where WhatsApp is widely used and for quick, informal engagement.

## Overview

Use WhatsApp for:
- Quick follow-ups and responses
- Personal, conversational outreach
- Markets with high WhatsApp adoption
- Time-sensitive communications

## Connecting WhatsApp

### Requirements

- Active WhatsApp Business account
- Phone with WhatsApp installed
- Stable internet connection

### Connection Steps

1. Navigate to **Settings** ‚Üí **Channels** ‚Üí **WhatsApp**
2. Click **Connect WhatsApp**
3. Scan the QR code with your WhatsApp app
4. Wait for connection confirmation
5. Your account appears as connected

::: info
Use WhatsApp Business for better features and compliance with WhatsApp's commercial messaging policies.
:::

## Message Best Practices

### Keep It Conversational
WhatsApp is personal - write like you're texting a colleague:

**Good:**
```
Hi {{name}}! Quick question - are you still looking to improve
your lead generation? We have some interesting insights to share.
```

**Avoid:**
```
Dear Sir/Madam, I am writing to introduce our comprehensive
lead generation solution which offers multiple features...
```

### Be Concise
- Get to the point quickly
- Use short paragraphs
- Avoid long blocks of text

### Respect Timing
- Consider time zones
- Avoid late night/early morning
- Business hours preferred

### Use Appropriate Tone
- Friendly but professional
- Match the recipient's style
- Avoid excessive formality

## Compliance

### WhatsApp Business Policy

To stay compliant:
- Only message people who have a business relationship
- Respond to opt-out requests immediately
- Don't send spam or unsolicited marketing
- Use approved message templates

### Best Practices for Compliance

1. **Get consent** - Only message leads who expect to hear from you
2. **Honor opt-outs** - Remove people who ask to stop receiving messages
3. **Be transparent** - Identify yourself and your company
4. **Provide value** - Every message should offer something useful

## Troubleshooting

### Connection Issues

**WhatsApp won't connect:**
- Ensure phone has active internet
- Try scanning QR code again
- Check WhatsApp is updated
- Restart WhatsApp app

**Connection drops frequently:**
- Keep phone connected to stable WiFi
- Don't use WhatsApp Web on other devices simultaneously
- Check phone battery optimization settings

### Message Delivery Issues

**Messages not delivering:**
- Verify recipient number is correct
- Check if recipient has blocked you
- Review daily limit usage
- Ensure account is in good standing

**Low response rates:**
- Improve message personalization
- Test different message styles
- Verify contact quality
- Consider timing

### Account Restrictions

**If your account gets restricted:**
1. Stop all automated messaging immediately
2. Review WhatsApp's terms of service
3. Appeal through WhatsApp if applicable
4. Wait for restriction to lift
5. Improve compliance before resuming
