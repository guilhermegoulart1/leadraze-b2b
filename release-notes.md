Bug Fixes

LinkedIn Premium Badge Normalization

Fixed the display of LinkedIn Premium badges on CRM cards. Badges were inconsistent and taking up too much space. They now render in a standardized, compact format.

Google Maps Credits Consumption

Fixed an issue where some Google Maps campaigns were incorrectly consuming AI credits instead of Google Maps credits. Credit deductions are now applied correctly based on campaign type.

CRM Card Profile Link Removal

Removed the "Profile" link from CRM cards to standardize the card layout and maintain visual consistency across the interface.

AI Salesperson Creation Layout

Improved the layout of the AI Salesperson creation flow for a more intuitive user experience. The interface now supports multiple niches and guides users through configuration more clearly.

AI Salesperson Edit Functionality

Added the ability to edit name and profile picture for AI Salespeople. Customize your agents' identity at any time after creation.

New Features

New Conversation Window for Agent Setup

Introducing a redesigned conversation window for adding new agents. The new interface is more intuitive and streamlined, making agent configuration faster and easier.

Google Maps Data Enrichment Scraper

Automatically enrich Google Maps campaign leads with data extracted from their websites. The scraper pulls additional business information to create more complete lead profiles.

AI Website Analysis Agent

A specialized AI agent analyzes website data from Google Maps leads to generate business insights. Get intelligent summaries and relevant information about each collected company automatically.

Google Maps Campaign Reports

A new dedicated reporting page for Google Maps campaigns. View detailed analytics, lead metrics, and campaign performance in one centralized dashboard.

Map and Official Business Data in Contacts

Contact records now display an embedded map and official business information. Visualize lead locations and access verified company data directly from the contact profile.

Multiple Emails and Phone Numbers Support

The scraper now captures and stores multiple email addresses and phone numbers when found. All available contact methods are saved to the lead record for comprehensive outreach options.

Beta: Intelligence Center (Secret Agents)

Introducing a collection of specialized scrapers designed to discover niches, companies, people, and connections for prospecting. Build targeted lead lists with advanced intelligence gathering tools. As a beta feature, we welcome your feedback to help shape its development.
